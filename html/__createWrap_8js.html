Creates a function that either curries or invokes func with optional this binding and partially applied arguments.

Parameters
{Function|string}func The function or method name to wrap.
{number}bitmask The bitmask flags. 1 - _.bind 2 - _.bindKey 4 - _.curry or _.curryRight of a bound function 8 - _.curry 16 - _.curryRight 32 - _.partial 64 - _.partialRight 128 - _.rearg 256 - _.ary 512 - _.flip
{*}[thisArg] The this binding of func.
{Array}[partials] The arguments to be partially applied.
{Array}[holders] The partials placeholder indexes.
{Array}[argPos] The argument positions of the new function.
{number}[ary] The arity cap of func.
{number}[arity] The arity of func.
Returns
{Function} Returns the new wrapped function.
