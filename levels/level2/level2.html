Level 2 - Key To Success

Tasks

1. Using move_up, move_down, move_right, move_left and get_direction() to reach destination.
2. At each junction you can see keys, the keys will tell you where to go. Use get_direction() function it returns 1, 2 and 3.
3. 1 means you have to go up, 2 means you have to go down and 3 means you have entered wrong zone which means your code is wrong.
4. At last if you reach correct destination you will win the game.
5. In this level you dont have to collect all stones you have to go to the the correct reality stone.
