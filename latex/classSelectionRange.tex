\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
A single selection range. 

When \href{https://codemirror.net/6/docs/ref/\#state.EditorState^allowMultipleSelections}{\texttt{ {\ttfamily allow\+Multiple\+Selections}}} is enabled, a \href{https://codemirror.net/6/docs/ref/\#state.EditorSelection}{\texttt{ selection}} may hold multiple ranges. By default, selections hold exactly one range.
