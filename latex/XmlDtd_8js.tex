Represents an X\+ML document type definition (D\+TD). 

A document type definition is structured as follows, where {\ttfamily \{name\}} is the name of the D\+TD, {\ttfamily \{sys\+Id\}} is the system identifier of the D\+TD, {\ttfamily \{pub\+Id\}} is the public identifier of the D\+TD, and {\ttfamily \{int\+Subset\}} is the internal subset of the D\+TD\+:


\begin{DoxyCode}{0}
\DoxyCodeLine{<!\textcolor{keyword}{DOCTYPE} \{\textcolor{keyword}{name}\} \textcolor{keyword}{SYSTEM} \textcolor{stringliteral}{"\{sysId\}"} \textcolor{keyword}{PUBLIC} \textcolor{stringliteral}{"\{pubId\}"} [}
\DoxyCodeLine{    \{\textcolor{keyword}{intSubset}\}}
\DoxyCodeLine{]>}
\end{DoxyCode}
